Budhathoki appointed as acting CEO of Green Dev Bank

Hari Bahadur Budhathoki has been appointed as the Acting CEO of Green Development Bank Limited. Earlier, he was Head of Credit and Business Growth in the bank. He has 16 years of experience in the banking sector. The position of CEO in the bank was vacant after the end of the tenure of Umesh Krishna Acharya.
